Planning Your Visit

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather across most of India, making it ideal for cultural exploration. However, specific festivals may occur throughout the year.

Regional Variations

Festival dates may vary by region and follow different calendars (lunar, solar, etc.). Always confirm local dates when planning your trip.

Accommodations

Book accommodations well in advance for major festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Durga Puja as they attract large numbers of visitors.
